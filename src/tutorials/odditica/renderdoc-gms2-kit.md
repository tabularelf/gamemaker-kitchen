RenderDoc is a GPU debugging utility that allows you to inspect what the GPU is doing during gameplay. It is an industry standard tool and is extremely useful for diagnosing all sorts of problems, including performance bottlenecks and shader glitches. This brief tutorial covers how to how up RenderDoc to your GameMaker games.
